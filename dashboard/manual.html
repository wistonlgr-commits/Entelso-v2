WhatsApp Bot Guide (Technicians)

Welcome to the Entelso inventory system. To make your field work easier, you don't need to install any app. The entire system is managed by sending messages directly to our WhatsApp Bot.

Your Security PIN (6 digits)
For any action that modifies an equipment's status (such as taking it to a site, reporting damage, or uploading photos), the bot will ask for your PIN. Never share it with anyone; this code automatically registers your name as the person responsible for the action.

What can you do with the Bot?

1. Check the location or status of an equipment

If you need to know where a tool is or if it's available, simply ask the bot.

  • What to write: "Where is equipment DW-20V-459?" or "Find equipment 27367".
  • Requirement: You just need to mention the equipment's serial number or ID.

2. Assign an equipment to yourself or move it

When you take an equipment from the warehouse to a job site, you must notify the system.

  • What to write: "I'm taking equipment 27367 to the NSW zone, my PIN is 123456".
  • Requirement: Equipment Number, Destination Zone, and your 6-digit PIN.
  • Note: If you forget any detail, don't worry, the bot will ask you for it.

3. Report damaged or maintenance equipment

If a tool fails or breaks in the field, report it immediately so it won't be assigned to anyone else.

  • What to write: "Equipment DW-20V has a stripped cable and won't turn on. PIN 123456".
  • Requirement: Equipment Number, a brief description of the failure, and your PIN.

4. Register a new equipment into the system

If you receive a new tool that isn't in the system yet, you can register it yourself.

  • What to write: "I want to register a new equipment. ID: 88899, Description: Cordless Drill".
  • Requirement: The Equipment ID or Serial Number and the tool's Description.

5. Upload photos of the tool

It is mandatory and highly recommended to leave visual evidence of the condition in which you deliver or receive an equipment.

  • What to do: Open the bot chat and send the photo. In the message attached to the photo (caption), write: "Photo of equipment 27367, PIN 123456".
  • Requirement: Send the photo along with the Equipment Number and your PIN in the image caption.
  • Note: You can send multiple photos at once. The bot has memory, so it will only ask for your details on the first photo and assume the rest belong to the same equipment.

Practical Tips

  • Natural language: The bot understands human language. You don't need to memorize strange commands.
  • Text and photos only: The bot cannot listen to voice notes or read PDF/Word documents.
  • QR Code: If the equipment has a QR code label, scanning it with your camera will send you directly to the chat to interact.

Control Panel (Web Dashboard)

Welcome to the Entelso Administrative Dashboard. This platform is your main control center to have complete visibility of the company's assets, equipment, and tools in real time.

1. Main View & Indicators

Upon entering the platform, the top section gives you an instant summary:

  • Key Metrics: You will see the percentage of available equipment and how many are currently in maintenance.
  • Quick Filters: You can filter the main table by Status, Zone (Location), or Work Team.
  • Search Bar: Use the search bar to instantly find equipment by their serial number or description.

2. Individual Equipment Management (Asset Profile)

If you click on any equipment in the list, the Details Side Panel will open on the right.

Details Side Panel

  • QR Code: From here you can generate, print, and download the QR code to physically stick on the tool.
  • Photo Gallery: Visual evidence of the equipment sent by technicians. Click on the photos to view them in full size.
  • Activity History: An unalterable timeline that shows exactly who moved the equipment, where to, and on what date.

Edit an Equipment

Inside the Side Panel, at the top right you will find the Pencil (Edit) icon. By clicking it, you can:

  • Update critical dates (Last and next calibration or Test/Tag).
  • Manually reassign the equipment to another technician or zone (in case the technician didn't do it via WhatsApp).
  • Photo Management: At the bottom of the window, you can upload new photos or delete old ones by clicking the red "X" on the image.
Don't forget to save
Always remember to press the blue "Save Changes" button when finished to save your edits to the database.

3. Bulk Actions

To save time when managing dozens of tools at once, the system allows mass actions.

  1. Select the ☑ checkboxes on the left side of the equipment in the main table.
  2. The "Bulk Actions" bar will appear above the table.
  3. From there you can simultaneously update the Category, Zone, Status, Team, or even Delete all selected equipment with a single click.

4. Create New Equipment

While technicians can enter basic tools from WhatsApp, as an administrator you can do it more comprehensively:

  1. Click the blue "Add Asset" button in the top right corner of the dashboard.
  2. Fill out the equipment's technical profile (Serial Number, Description, Dates, Status, and Zone) and save it.

5. Real-Time WhatsApp Integration

Remember that your field technicians update this platform without needing a web user account. Every time a technician reports a failure, assigns an equipment, or uploads a photo via the WhatsApp bot, you will see it reflected automatically in your table and in the equipment's history.
